Join us at Moldova  ICT Summit 2021

10th edition of the event will discuss how technologies bring impact and help build better experiences for the whole society, what is the role of the ICT sector in the country’s digital transformation, the future of digital for Government, Business, Academia.
31

speakers

7

sessions

This Summit will emphasize Government and Business joining forces for the digital future to be instrumental in resetting the economy, developing digital culture, and driving positive change. It will provide the dialog platform for the stakeholders in the transformation journey, present existing and foster new partnerships, where technology brings solutions for the most complex national issues.
